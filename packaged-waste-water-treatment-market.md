<p><p>The packaged wastewater treatment market includes several technologies designed for efficient waste management. Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) combines biological treatment with microfiltration, producing high-quality effluents. Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR) utilizes biofilm attached to moving media for improved treatment efficiency. Sequencing Batch Reactor (SBR) operates in batch cycles for effective solid-liquid separation and nutrient removal. Extended Aeration systems offer prolonged aeration time for enhanced biological decomposition, catering to various wastewater types and treatment needs while ensuring environmental compliance.</p></p>
