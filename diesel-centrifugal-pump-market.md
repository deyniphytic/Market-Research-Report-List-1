<p><strong>The Diesel Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel centrifugal pump market is categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Axial Flow Pumps** transport fluids along the pump shaft, suitable for high flow rates and low pressure applications. </p><p>2. **Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps** combine features of both axial and centrifugal pumps, providing versatility for various applications. </p><p>3. **Peripheral Pumps** deliver low flow rates at high pressures, ideal for specific tasks like irrigation. </p><p>4. **Jet Pumps** utilize a jet of fluid to create suction, effective for well water extraction and boosting pressure.</p></p>
