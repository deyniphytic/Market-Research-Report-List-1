<p><strong>The Squeeze Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Squeeze Casting Machine market is divided into two primary types: Hot Chamber Squeeze Casting Machines and Cold Chamber Squeeze Casting Machines. Hot Chamber machines are designed for non-ferrous metals with a common die-casting application, allowing molten metal to be injected into a mold under high pressure, enhancing metal flow and reducing defects. In contrast, Cold Chamber machines operate by pouring molten metal into a chamber separate from the injection mechanism, suitable for materials with high melting points, ensuring optimal casting quality and precision.</p></p>
