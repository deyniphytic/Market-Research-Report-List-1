<p><p>Online food delivery platforms can be categorized into three main types: restaurant-controlled, independent, and other markets. Restaurant-controlled platforms are operated directly by restaurants, allowing them to manage orders and delivery in-house, ensuring brand consistency. Independent platforms serve as intermediaries, connecting multiple restaurants with customers while handling logistics and payments. Other markets may encompass niche services, like meal kits or specialty cuisine deliveries, catering to specific consumer needs beyond traditional restaurant offerings. Each type offers distinct advantages and challenges in the evolving food delivery landscape.</p></p>
