<p><p>The battery grade solvents market includes various key types essential for lithium-ion battery production. Dimethyl Carbonate (DMC) is favored for its high purity and low toxicity, enhancing electrolyte performance. Ethyl Methyl Carbonate (EMC) offers excellent electrode compatibility and stability. Ethylene Carbonate (EC) serves as a critical solvent for improved thermal stability. Diethyl Carbonate (DEC) provides efficient solvation properties, while Propylene Carbonate (PC) is known for its high dielectric constant and ability to dissolve salts effectively. Together, these solvents optimize battery efficiency and safety.</p></p>
