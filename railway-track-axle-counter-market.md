<p><strong>The Railway Track Axle Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rail Side Mounting</li><li>On-rail Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Railway Track Axle Counter market features two primary types: Rail Side Mounting and On-Rail Mounting. Rail Side Mounting involves the installation of axle counters alongside the tracks, allowing for accurate train counting and monitoring from a secure position. In contrast, On-Rail Mounting integrates the axle counter directly onto the rail, enabling closer proximity to train movements and enhancing real-time data accuracy. Both types serve the essential function of improving safety and efficiency in railway operations.</p></p>
