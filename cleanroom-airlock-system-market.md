<p><p>The Cleanroom Airlock System market can be categorized into several types, each serving unique functions in contamination control. The Cascading Airlock features a series of interlocked chambers to ensure minimal particle transfer between environments. The Bubble Airlock utilizes a positive pressure differential, creating a 'bubble' of clean air for personnel and material transfer. The Sink Airlock employs a downward airflow to prevent the escape of contaminants. Other types may include sliding door airlocks and rapid transfer ports, catering to specific industry requirements.</p></p>
