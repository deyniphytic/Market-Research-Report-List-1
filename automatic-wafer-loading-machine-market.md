<p><ul><li>Inline</li><li>Offline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Wafer Loading Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: inline and offline machines. Inline machines are integrated into the production line, facilitating real-time loading of wafers during the manufacturing process, thereby enhancing efficiency and throughput. In contrast, offline machines operate independently, allowing for batch processing and flexibility in handling wafer loading without interrupting the main production flow. Each type serves distinct operational needs, with inline machines prioritizing speed and automation, while offline machines emphasize versatility and handling capacity.</p></p>
