<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Voltage Harmonic Filter market is categorized into two primary types: active and passive filters. Active filters use advanced electronic technology to dynamically adjust and eliminate harmonic disturbances in real-time, making them highly effective for varying loads. In contrast, passive filters rely on passive components like inductors, capacitors, and resistors to reduce harmonics, providing a cost-effective solution but with limited adaptability. Both types serve to enhance power quality, improve energy efficiency, and protect electrical systems from harmonic distortion.</p></p>
