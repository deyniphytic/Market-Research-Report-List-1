<p><p>The shochu market is primarily divided into two types based on the distillation process: multiply distilled and singly distilled. Multiply distilled shochu, often using column distillation, results in a higher alcohol content and a lighter flavor profile, appealing to those seeking a versatile spirit for mixing. On the other hand, singly distilled shochu retains more of the original ingredients’ character, offering a richer taste and complexity, making it favored by purists who appreciate traditional flavors and nuances.</p></p>
