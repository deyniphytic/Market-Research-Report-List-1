<p><ul><li>Topographic LiDAR</li><li>Bathymetric LiDAR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airborne LiDAR System market is primarily divided into two types: topographic and bathymetric LiDAR. Topographic LiDAR is used for land-based applications, capturing elevation data and creating detailed terrain models, useful in forestry, urban planning, and environmental studies. In contrast, bathymetric LiDAR is designed for underwater mapping, utilizing specific wavelengths to penetrate water surfaces, enabling the mapping of seabeds and coastal features. Both types serve distinct environmental monitoring and resource management needs, enhancing data accuracy and accessibility.</p></p>
