<p><p>Headliner (OE) refers to the interior fabric covering of vehicles' roofs, enhancing aesthetics and insulation. The market is divided into two primary types: thermoplastics and thermosets. Thermoplastic headliners are characterized by their ability to be reshaped when heated, offering flexibility and recyclability. In contrast, thermoset materials harden permanently after curing, providing durability and strength but limited reusability. Each type caters to specific automotive design needs, balancing performance, cost, and environmental considerations in the manufacturing process.</p></p>
