<p><p>The autonomous vehicle market encompasses various vehicle types, each with distinct powertrains. Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) rely solely on electric power, offering zero emissions. Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles utilize traditional fuel, remaining prevalent despite environmental concerns. Fuel Cell Electric Vehicles (FCEVs) generate electricity from hydrogen, producing only water vapor as a byproduct, promoting a cleaner alternative to conventional fuels.</p></p>
