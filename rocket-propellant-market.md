<p><p>The rocket propellant market comprises four primary types: </p><p>1. **Solid Rocket Propellant**: Utilizes a solid mixture of fuel and oxidizer, offering simplicity and reliability, commonly used in military and space launch vehicles. </p><p>2. **Liquid Rocket Propellant**: Involves separate components of fuel and oxidizer, allowing for variable thrust and precise control; widely used in spacecraft and launch vehicles. </p><p>3. **Gaseous Rocket Propellant**: Uses gaseous fuels, often in combination with liquid oxidizers, suitable for specific applications like upper stages. </p><p>4. **Hybrid Rocket Propellant**: Combines solid fuel with liquid or gaseous oxidizers, balancing performance and safety for diverse applications.</p></p>
