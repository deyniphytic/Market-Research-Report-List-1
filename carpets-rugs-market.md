<p><p>The carpets and rugs market comprises several key types. Tufted carpets are created by inserting yarn through a backing material, offering a soft texture. Woven carpets are interlaced on looms, providing durability and intricate designs. Needle-punched carpets use barbed needles to mat fibers together, resulting in a sturdy, dense surface. Knotted carpets are formed by hand-knotting yarn, known for their craftsmanship and longevity. Other types may include flatweaves and shag rugs, catering to diverse consumer preferences and styles.</p></p>
