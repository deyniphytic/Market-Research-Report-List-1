<p><p>The water purifier and filter market encompasses various purification technologies. UV purification filters use ultraviolet light to eliminate bacteria and viruses, ensuring safe drinking water. RO (Reverse Osmosis) purification filters employ a semipermeable membrane to remove dissolved impurities and contaminants. Gravity-based purification filters rely on natural gravitational force to filter water through various media, making them low-maintenance. Additionally, other purification methods include activated carbon filters, ceramic filters, and ion exchange, addressing diverse consumer needs for clean and safe drinking water.</p></p>
