<p><p>The Flex PCB market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Single-Sided Circuit**: Features a single conductive layer, ideal for simple applications. </p><p>2. **Double-Sided Circuit**: Contains copper patterns on both sides, allowing for more complex designs. </p><p>3. **Multi-Layer Circuit**: Consists of multiple layers, enhancing functionality and ensuring compactness. </p><p>4. **Rigid-Flex Circuit**: Combines flexible and rigid materials, offering durability and design flexibility for intricate electronic systems. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like consumer electronics and automotive.</p></p>
