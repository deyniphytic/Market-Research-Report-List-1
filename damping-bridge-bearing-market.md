<p><ul><li>Fixed Type</li><li>Guided Type</li><li>Free Sliding Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The damping bridge bearing market comprises three main types: Fixed Type, Guided Type, and Free Sliding Type. Fixed Type bearings provide stability by restraining movement, ideal for heavy-load applications. Guided Type bearings allow controlled movement in specific directions, accommodating slight shifts while maintaining bridge integrity. Free Sliding Type bearings enable unrestricted horizontal movement, ideal for expansion and contraction due to temperature changes. Each type serves distinct roles in enhancing bridge performance and longevity while ensuring safety and structural efficiency.</p></p>
