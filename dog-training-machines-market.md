<p><p>The dog training machines market features three primary types: Sound Mode, Vibration Mode, and Electrostatic Pulse Mode. Sound Mode utilizes auditory signals to capture a dog's attention and correct behavior. Vibration Mode employs mild vibrations as a tactile cue, encouraging focus without harsh punishment. Electrostatic Pulse Mode delivers a brief, low-level electrical stimulation as a corrective measure, aimed at reinforcing commands. Each mode caters to different training needs, ensuring effective communication between the handler and the dog while promoting obedience.</p></p>
