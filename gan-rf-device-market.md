<p><p>The GaN RF device market is categorized into three primary types based on the substrate materials used. GaN-on-Si offers cost advantages and compatibility with existing silicon processes, making it suitable for consumer applications. GaN-on-SiC provides superior thermal performance and efficiency, ideal for high-power and high-frequency applications like telecommunications and radar systems. GaN-on-Diamond, though less common, promises exceptional heat dissipation and is emerging for specialized applications, particularly in defense and aerospace industries, where performance and reliability are critical.</p></p>
