<p><ul><li>Optical Dual Absolute Encoder</li><li>Magnetic Dual Absolute Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual absolute encoder market comprises two main types: optical and magnetic encoders. Optical dual absolute encoders utilize light-based technology to provide high-resolution position feedback, ideal for precision applications. They are known for their accuracy and reliability but can be sensitive to dust and vibration. In contrast, magnetic dual absolute encoders use magnetic fields for position detection, offering robustness and resilience in harsh environments. Both types serve critical roles in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, ensuring precise positional control.</p></p>
