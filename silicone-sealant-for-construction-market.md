<p><p>Silicone sealant for construction is primarily classified into two market types: neutral cure and moisture cure. Neutral cure silicone is designed to release minimal odor and cure without producing corrosive byproducts, making it suitable for sensitive materials such as metals and stone. Conversely, moisture cure silicone relies on humidity to cure, forming a strong bond that is ideal for indoor and outdoor applications where moisture is present. Both types offer flexibility, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, enhancing construction longevity.</p></p>
