<p><p>The bench hydraulic press market is primarily categorized into H-Type and C-Type presses. H-Type presses feature a robust H-frame design, ensuring enhanced stability and support for heavy-duty applications like metal forming and assembly. In contrast, C-Type presses have a C-frame design, offering a wider open front for easier access, making them ideal for lighter applications, such as small-scale fabrication and assembly tasks. Both types serve distinct purposes based on the required strength, size, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
