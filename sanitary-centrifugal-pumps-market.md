<p><p>Sanitary centrifugal pumps are designed for hygienic applications, often used in food, pharmaceutical, and biotechnology industries. They typically come with two seal types: single mechanical seal and double mechanical seal. Single mechanical seals are simpler and cost-effective, suitable for low-pressure applications, while double mechanical seals provide enhanced leak protection and are ideal for high-pressure or hazardous environments. The choice between the two primarily depends on the specific application requirements and the level of safety needed for the process.</p></p>
