<p><p>The Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) motors market is categorized into three segments: small power, middle power, and high power. Small power VFD motors typically range from a few watts to several kilowatts, serving applications like fans and small pumps. Middle power motors, usually ranging from 10 to 100 kilowatts, cater to more demanding tasks in manufacturing and HVAC. High power VFD motors exceed 100 kilowatts and are used in heavy industries such as mining, oil, and gas, where significant torque and efficiency are required.</p></p>
