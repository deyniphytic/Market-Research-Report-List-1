<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters are critical in signal processing, primarily used in mobile devices and telecommunications. The market for SAW filters is categorized into two main types: transversal filters and resonator filters. Transversal filters utilize multiple transducers to create a filter response, effectively enhancing selectivity and performance. In contrast, resonator filters rely on the resonance of acoustic waves to achieve narrow bandwidths and high quality factors. Both types are essential for achieving efficient frequency filtering in various applications.</p></p>
