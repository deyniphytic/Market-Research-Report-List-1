<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) system market includes several key components. Hard tags are rigid devices attached to items for theft prevention, typically removed at the point of sale. Soft tags are flexible labels that can be affixed to products, offering a discreet security solution. Detachers are tools used to remove hard tags once a purchase is made. Detection systems consist of antennas or sensors placed at store exits to alert staff of unauthorized item removal, enhancing overall security.</p></p>
