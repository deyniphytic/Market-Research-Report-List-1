<p><p>The cordless assembly fastening tools market is divided primarily into two types: electric and pneumatic. Electric type tools are battery-powered, offering convenience and portability for various fastening applications. They are suitable for both light and heavy-duty tasks, often featuring adjustable torque settings. Pneumatic type tools, on the other hand, rely on compressed air, delivering high power and speed ideal for industrial environments. While they provide consistent performance, they require an air compressor and are typically less portable than electric options.</p></p>
