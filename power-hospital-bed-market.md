<p><p>The Power Hospital Bed Market is primarily categorized into two types: Full Electric Hospital Beds and Semi-Electric Beds. Full Electric Beds feature electronic controls for adjusting the head, foot, and overall height, offering maximum convenience for both patients and caregivers. Conversely, Semi-Electric Beds allow for positional adjustments using electric controls for the head and foot sections, while manual cranking is employed for height adjustments. Both types enhance patient comfort and facilitate easier care for medical staff.</p></p>
