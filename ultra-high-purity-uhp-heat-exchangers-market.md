<p><p>The Ultra High Purity (UHP) heat exchangers market comprises primarily two types: shell & tube and immersion heat exchangers. Shell & tube UHP heat exchangers feature a series of tubes contained within a shell, optimizing heat transfer while minimizing contamination risks. Immersion type heat exchangers directly immerse heating or cooling elements, ensuring efficient thermal exchange with minimal space requirements. Both designs serve critical applications in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, where process purity and precise temperature control are essential.</p></p>
