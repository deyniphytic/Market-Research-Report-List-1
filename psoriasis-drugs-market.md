<p><p>The psoriasis drugs market is categorized into three main types: Tumor Necrosis Factor (TNF) Inhibitors, Interleukin Inhibitors, and Others. TNF inhibitors target inflammatory pathways by blocking the action of tumor necrosis factor, reducing skin inflammation. Interleukin inhibitors focus on specific interleukins involved in the immune response, offering targeted therapy for psoriasis. The "Others" category encompasses various treatments, including topical agents, systemic therapies, and phototherapy, catering to diverse patient needs and varying disease severity.</p></p>
