<p><p>The specialty geomembranes market comprises primarily two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE geomembranes are known for their strength and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for landfill liners, environmental containment, and mining applications. In contrast, LDPE geomembranes offer enhanced flexibility and are often used in applications requiring easy handling, such as agricultural liners and water containment. Both types play essential roles in environmental protection and resource management across various industries.</p></p>
