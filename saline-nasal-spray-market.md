<p><strong>The Saline Nasal Spray Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isotonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li><li>Hypertonic Sea Water Nasal Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The saline nasal spray market comprises two primary types: isotonic and hypertonic sea water nasal sprays. Isotonic sea water nasal sprays contain a salt concentration similar to that of body fluids, providing gentle hydration and relief for maintaining nasal moisture. In contrast, hypertonic sea water nasal sprays have a higher salt concentration, which can help reduce swelling and promote mucus clearance by drawing water out of swollen tissues. Both types are popular for managing nasal congestion and enhancing overall respiratory health.</p></p>
