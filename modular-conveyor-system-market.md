<p><p>The modular conveyor system market includes various types tailored for specific applications. Roller conveyors utilize rollers to move items, ideal for heavy loads and continuous transport. Belt conveyors feature a continuous belt that carries materials, suitable for diverse products. Overhead conveyors utilize suspended tracks for transporting items, maximizing floor space. Pallet conveyors are designed for handling pallets, enhancing efficiency in warehousing. Other types may include chain and magnetic conveyors, addressing specialized operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
