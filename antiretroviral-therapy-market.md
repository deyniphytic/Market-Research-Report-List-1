<p><strong>The Antiretroviral Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NNRTIs</li><li>NRTI</li><li>Multiclass Combination Products</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiretroviral therapy market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **NRTIs (Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors)** block reverse transcriptase, preventing viral replication. </p><p>2. **NNRTIs (Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors)** also target reverse transcriptase but act in a different manner, offering alternative mechanisms. </p><p>3. **Integrase Inhibitors** interfere with the integration of viral DNA into the host genome, hindering replication. </p><p>4. **Multiclass Combination Products** combine various antiretroviral agents, enhancing efficacy and adherence by simplifying treatment regimens for HIV patients.</p></p>
