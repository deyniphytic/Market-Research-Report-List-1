<p><p>The pre-fabricated cleanroom market comprises two main types: softwall and hardwall cleanrooms. Pre-fabricated softwall cleanrooms feature flexible, removable walls made of soft materials, allowing for easy customization and mobility, making them ideal for temporary setups or smaller operations. In contrast, hardwall cleanrooms have rigid, durable walls that provide greater structural integrity and enhanced contamination control, suitable for long-term use in industries like pharmaceuticals and biotechnology. Both types offer efficient, controlled environments tailored to specific cleanroom standards.</p></p>
