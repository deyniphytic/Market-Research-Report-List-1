<p><ul><li>Counter Top Fryer</li><li>Floor-standing Fryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial electric fryer market is primarily categorized into two types: countertop fryers and floor-standing fryers. Countertop fryers are compact and designed for limited spaces, making them ideal for small establishments such as cafes and food trucks. They are easy to operate and maintain. In contrast, floor-standing fryers are larger, capable of handling higher volumes of food, and are suited for busy restaurants and commercial kitchens. Both types are vital for efficient frying operations, catering to different business needs and capacities.</p></p>
