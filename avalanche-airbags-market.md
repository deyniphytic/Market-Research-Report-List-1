<p><p>The Avalanche Airbags market features two primary types: Mono and Dual Avalanche Airbags. Mono Avalanche Airbags consist of a single air chamber designed to deploy and create an inflated cushion around the user, enhancing buoyancy during an avalanche. In contrast, Dual Avalanche Airbags have two separate air chambers, offering increased stability and improved performance in unpredictable conditions. Both types aim to mitigate risks associated with avalanche scenarios, enhancing safety for backcountry enthusiasts and professionals in extreme environments.</p></p>
