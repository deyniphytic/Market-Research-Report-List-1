<p><p>The paper strength enhancer market comprises two main types: wet strength agents and dry strength agents. Wet strength agents are added during the papermaking process to enhance the paper's ability to retain strength when wet, improving durability and performance in applications like tissue and packaging. Dry strength agents, on the other hand, are used to increase paper strength after drying, focusing on enhancing tensile and tear strength. Both types are crucial for producing high-quality, functional paper products.</p></p>
