<p><strong>The HVAC Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li><li>Absolute Pressure Sensors</li><li>Gauge Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC pressure sensors market comprises three main types: differential pressure sensors, absolute pressure sensors, and gauge pressure sensors. Differential pressure sensors measure the difference between two pressure points, essential for monitoring airflow and filter conditions. Absolute pressure sensors evaluate pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, crucial for applications requiring precise pressure measurements. Gauge pressure sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, typically used for tasks like maintaining ductwork and monitoring system performance, ensuring efficient HVAC operations.</p></p>
