<p><p>High-Pressure Processing (HPP) machines are innovative equipment used in the food and beverage industry to enhance food safety, extend shelf life, and preserve flavors and nutritional content. This technology is increasingly favored for its ability to maintain the integrity of food while providing a clean label, aligning with consumer preferences for natural ingredients.</p><p>The HPP machine market is experiencing significant growth, anticipated to expand at a CAGR of 14% during the forecast period. Additionally, advancements in HPP technology, including improved efficiency and reduced operational costs, are making these machines more accessible to smaller manufacturers.
