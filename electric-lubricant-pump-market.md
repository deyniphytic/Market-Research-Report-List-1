<p><ul><li>Under Load</li><li>Self-priming</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric lubricant pump market comprises three main types: Under Load, Self-Priming, and Submersible pumps. Under Load pumps are designed to efficiently provide lubrication under high-pressure conditions, ensuring optimal performance in demanding applications. Self-Priming pumps can draw lubricant from a lower level without manual intervention, making them convenient for various setups. Submersible pumps operate submerged in lubricant, ideal for applications where space is limited, offering reliable and consistent lubrication in environments like automotive and industrial sectors.</p></p>
