<p><p>Building materials software is categorized into two main types: On-Premises and Cloud-Based. On-Premises solutions are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering complete control over data and security, but often requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based software is hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier access from various devices. This model typically involves a subscription fee, reducing initial costs and allowing for automatic updates and collaborative features, making it ideal for dynamic construction environments.</p></p>
