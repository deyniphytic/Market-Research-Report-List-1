<p><strong>The Surface Profile Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stylus Surface Profiler</li><li>Non-contact Profiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surface profile measurement market is primarily divided into two types: stylus surface profilers and non-contact profilers. Stylus surface profilers utilize a fine probe that touches the surface to measure its texture and topography, providing high precision and resolution. Non-contact profilers, on the other hand, employ optical techniques, such as laser or white light, to measure surface profiles without physical interaction, allowing for analysis of soft or delicate materials without damage. Both technologies are essential for quality control across various industries.</p></p>
