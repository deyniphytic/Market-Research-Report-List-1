<p><ul><li>Soluble Oat Fiber</li><li>Insoluble Oat Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Oat Fiber market is divided into two primary types: soluble and insoluble oat fiber. Soluble oat fiber dissolves in water, forming a gel-like substance that aids in digestion and helps regulate blood sugar levels. It is commonly used in food products for its health benefits and texture-enhancing properties. Insoluble oat fiber, on the other hand, does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the diet, promoting regular bowel movements. Both types are valued for their nutritional advantages in health-conscious consumer products.</p></p>
