<p><p>The contactless readers market comprises two main types: fixed and mobile contactless readers. Fixed contactless readers are permanently installed at points of sale or access points, designed for high-volume transactions and secure environments. They facilitate quick, hands-free payments and entry. In contrast, mobile contactless readers are portable devices that enable merchants to accept payments on-the-go, making them ideal for small businesses and outdoor events. Both types enhance convenience and efficiency in various transaction scenarios.</p></p>
