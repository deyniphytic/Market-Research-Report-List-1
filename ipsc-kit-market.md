<p><p>The iPSC kit market comprises various technologies for generating induced pluripotent stem cells. Adenoviral and Sendai virus systems utilize viral vectors for efficient reprogramming of somatic cells. The PiggyBac system employs a transposon-based approach for stable integration, while minicircle vectors provide high expression with minimal genomic disruption. Episomal vectors enable non-integrative reprogramming with reduced risk of insertional mutagenesis. Other methods may include alternative plasmids or approaches, catering to diverse research and therapeutic applications in regenerative medicine.</p></p>
