<p><p>The HEV lithium-ion battery market comprises various types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (NMC) balances performance, energy density, and cost, making it popular in automotive applications. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) excels in energy density and performance for high-range applications. Lastly, Lithium Titanate Oxide (LTO) supports rapid charging and long life but has lower energy density.</p></p>
