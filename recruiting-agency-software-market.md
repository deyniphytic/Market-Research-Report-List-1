<p><p>Recruiting agency software is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, allowing agencies to manage recruitment processes online without heavy IT infrastructure. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on agency servers, providing greater control over data and customization but requiring upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different organizational needs, with cloud options favored for their ease of use, while on-premises options appeal to those seeking enhanced security and control.</p></p>
