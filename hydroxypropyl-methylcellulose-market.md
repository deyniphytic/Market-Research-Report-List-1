<p><p>Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose (HPMC) is a semisynthetic, inert, and viscoelastic polymer derived from cellulose. It is widely used as a thickener, binder, film-former, and stabilizer in various industries, including construction, pharmaceuticals, food, and personal care. HPMC is valued for its water retention, thermal gelation, and emulsifying properties, making it a versatile ingredient in products like adhesives, coatings, tablets, and cosmetics.</p><p>The Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in construction applications, particularly in cement-based products, where it enhances workability and water retention.
