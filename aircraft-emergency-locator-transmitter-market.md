<p><p>The Aircraft Emergency Locator Transmitter (ELT) market comprises two primary types: embedded and portable systems. Embedded ELTs are integrated into the aircraft's structure, providing a streamlined solution that activates automatically upon impact, ensuring quick location by rescue teams. On the other hand, portable ELTs are standalone devices easily carried by crew or passengers, offering flexibility and usability in various emergency situations. Both types are crucial for enhancing aircraft safety and improving survival rates during accidents.</p></p>
