<p><ul><li>Absolute Gravity Gradiometer</li><li>The Relative Gravity Gradiometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Borehole gravity survey services include two main market types: absolute gravity gradiometers and relative gravity gradiometers. Absolute gravity gradiometers measure the gravitational acceleration at specific points, providing precise data about subsurface density variations, crucial for mineral and oil exploration. In contrast, relative gravity gradiometers detect differences in gravitational forces across locations, allowing for detailed mapping of geological structures. Both types play a vital role in enhancing the accuracy of geophysical surveys, influencing exploration and extraction strategies.</p></p>
