<p><p>The Automatic Guided System market is broadly categorized into three types: Magnetic, Electronic, and Laser systems. Magnetic guidance uses magnetic strips or markers for navigation, ensuring precise path following. Electronic systems leverage sensors and sophisticated control algorithms for real-time adjustments and obstacle avoidance. Laser-guided systems utilize laser beams for accurate positioning and navigation, often in complex environments. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the application, ranging from warehouse automation to complex industrial settings, enhancing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
