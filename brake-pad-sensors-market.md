<p><p>Brake pad sensors are crucial components in automotive safety, providing real-time monitoring of brake pad wear. The market can be categorized into two main types: mechanical and electrical sensors. Mechanical sensors typically utilize a physical component that makes contact with the rotor, triggering an alert when pads are worn. In contrast, electrical sensors employ electronic methods, often using resistance changes to detect wear levels. Both types enhance vehicle safety by alerting drivers when brake maintenance is necessary, ensuring optimal performance.</p></p>
