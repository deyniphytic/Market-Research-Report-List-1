<p><p>The precision spindle market comprises several types, primarily including rolling spindles, air bearing spindles, and others. Rolling spindles utilize ball or roller bearings for high-speed performance and load capacity, making them suitable for various machining applications. Air bearing spindles, on the other hand, employ a cushion of air to reduce friction and enable ultra-precise movements, ideal for advanced manufacturing processes. Other types may include liquid-cooled or hybrid spindles, catering to specific industry requirements and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
