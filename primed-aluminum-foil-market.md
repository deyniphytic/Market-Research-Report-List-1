<p><p>The primed aluminum foil market is categorized into two main types: water-based and oil-based. Water-based primed aluminum foils use water-soluble adhesives, making them environmentally friendly and suitable for a variety of applications, including food packaging and labeling. In contrast, oil-based primed foils utilize petroleum-derived adhesives, offering enhanced durability and moisture resistance, which is ideal for industrial uses. Each type serves distinct purposes based on adhesion properties and environmental considerations, catering to different industry demands.</p></p>
