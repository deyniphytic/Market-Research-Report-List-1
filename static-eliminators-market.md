<p><p>The static eliminators market comprises two main types: contact type and induction type static eliminators. Contact type static eliminators directly neutralize charges through physical contact with the material, effectively removing static from surfaces. In contrast, induction type static eliminators use electrostatic fields to neutralize charges without physical contact, making them suitable for delicate materials. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring enhanced product quality, safety, and process efficiency by mitigating static-related issues in manufacturing and packaging environments.</p></p>
