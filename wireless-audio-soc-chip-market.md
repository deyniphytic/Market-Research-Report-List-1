<p><p>The wireless audio SoC (System on Chip) market comprises primarily two types: Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Bluetooth SoCs are designed for short-range audio streaming, enabling portable devices like headphones and speakers to connect easily with smartphones and tablets, emphasizing low power consumption and compact design. In contrast, Wi-Fi SoCs facilitate higher-quality audio streaming over longer distances, suitable for home audio systems and smart speakers, offering better bandwidth for multi-device connectivity and supporting higher fidelity sound. Both technologies cater to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
