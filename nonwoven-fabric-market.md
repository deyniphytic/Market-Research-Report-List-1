<p><p>The nonwoven fabric market includes various types such as spun-bonded, needle-punched, and spiny nonwoven fabrics. Spun-bonded nonwovens are created by melting polymer fibers and laying them in a web, offering strength and durability. Needle-punched nonwovens are made by mechanically entangling fibers, enhancing thickness and insulation properties. Spiny nonwoven fabrics feature a unique texture due to specialized production processes, catering to specific applications. Other categories encompass various innovative nonwoven technologies, expanding the market's versatility and applications across industries.</p></p>
