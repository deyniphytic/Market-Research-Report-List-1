<p><p>The Passive Infrared Motion Sensor market encompasses various types including accelerometers, gyroscopes, magnetometers, and combo sensors. Accelerometers detect linear motion by measuring changes in velocity, while gyroscopes track angular movement and orientation. Magnetometers sense magnetic fields, often used for position referencing. Combo sensors integrate multiple functionalities, providing enhanced detection capabilities by combining data from accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers. Each type serves distinct applications, from security systems to wearable devices, enhancing user interaction and environment responsiveness.</p></p>
