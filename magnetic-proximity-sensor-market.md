<p><p>The magnetic proximity sensor market includes fixed distance and adjustable distance sensors. Fixed distance sensors operate at a predetermined range, offering consistency for applications where the distance between the sensor and the target remains unchanged. In contrast, adjustable distance sensors allow users to modify the sensing range to suit varying conditions, providing flexibility for different environments or requirements. Both types serve crucial roles in automation, security, and industrial processes by detecting the presence of magnetic objects effectively.</p></p>
