<p><strong>The GPON Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Line Terminal (OLT)</li><li>Optical Network Terminal (ONT)</li><li>Passive Optical Splitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) equipment market consists of three key components: Optical Line Terminals (OLT), Optical Network Terminals (ONT), and Passive Optical Splitters. OLTs serve as the central hub, managing data traffic between the provider and subscribers. ONTs are located at the customer premises, converting optical signals into usable data. Passive Optical Splitters allow a single fiber connection to be shared among multiple users, enhancing network efficiency. Together, these components facilitate high-speed broadband delivery in fiber-optic networks.</p></p>
