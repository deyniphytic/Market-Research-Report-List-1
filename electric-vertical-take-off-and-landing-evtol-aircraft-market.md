<p><strong>The Electric Vertical Take-off and Landing (eVTOL) Aircraft Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vectored Thrust</li><li>Multirotor</li><li>Lift + Cruise</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eVTOL aircraft market comprises three main types: vectored thrust, multirotor, and lift + cruise. Vectored thrust designs utilize angled rotor systems to achieve vertical lift and horizontal flight, offering high speed and efficiency. Multirotor aircraft feature multiple rotors for vertical takeoff and landing, providing stability and redundancy, making them suitable for urban air mobility. Lift + cruise configurations combine rotor-based lift for takeoff and fixed wings for cruising, optimizing speed and range, ideal for longer distances.</p></p>
