<p><p>The snow sports helmets market is primarily categorized into two materials: ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) and PC (Polycarbonate). ABS helmets are known for their durability and impact resistance, making them a popular choice for recreational activities. They offer a good balance of strength and weight, while being relatively cost-effective. Conversely, PC helmets are lighter and provide superior impact protection, making them ideal for professional or high-stakes sports. Each material caters to different user preferences and safety requirements in the snow sports arena.</p></p>
