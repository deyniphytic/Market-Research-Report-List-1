<p><p>The drying box market comprises various types, primarily including the electrothermal drying box and the vacuum drying box. Electrothermal drying boxes utilize electric heat to efficiently evaporate moisture from materials, making them ideal for laboratory and industrial applications. In contrast, vacuum drying boxes operate under reduced pressure, allowing for low-temperature drying, which preserves the quality of heat-sensitive substances. These variations cater to different drying needs across sectors such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and material science.</p></p>
