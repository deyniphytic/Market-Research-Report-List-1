<p><p>The real-time chlorine analyzer market is categorized into two main types: non-reagent type and reagent type chlorine analyzers. Non-reagent type analyzers measure chlorine levels without the need for chemical reagents, providing quick and continuous monitoring, making them cost-effective and low-maintenance. In contrast, reagent type analyzers utilize chemical reagents to react with chlorine, offering high accuracy and sensitivity. While reagent types may require more maintenance due to the need for reagents, they excel in precision for various applications in water treatment and industrial processes.</p></p>
