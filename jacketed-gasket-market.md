<p><p>The jacketed gasket market can be categorized into three main types based on material composition. Semi-metallic gaskets combine metal and non-metal materials, offering enhanced durability and resistance to temperature fluctuations, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Non-metallic gaskets, made primarily from materials like rubber or polymers, provide excellent sealing properties and are ideal for lower pressure systems. Metallic gaskets, constructed entirely of metal, are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, offering exceptional strength and reliability.</p></p>
