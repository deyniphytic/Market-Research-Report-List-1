<p><p>The metal bellows seals market is categorized into stationary and rotary types. Stationary seals are designed to seal fluid in fixed systems, ensuring minimal leakage and providing durability under various pressures and temperatures. In contrast, rotary seals are employed in applications with moving parts, such as pumps and compressors, to prevent leakage while accommodating dynamic movement. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing operational efficiency, extending equipment life, and reducing maintenance costs across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
