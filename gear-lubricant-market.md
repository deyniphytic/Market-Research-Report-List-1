<p><p>The Gear Lubricant Market includes various types tailored for specific applications. **Rust & Corrosion Inhibited** lubricants protect gears from moisture-induced damage, ideal for environments prone to water exposure. **Compounded** lubricants combine oils and fatty additives, offering enhanced film strength for heavy-duty, slow-speed applications. **Extreme Pressure (EP)** lubricants contain additives to withstand high stress and prevent wear under severe operating conditions, making them suitable for high-load, high-temperature environments. Each type ensures optimal gear performance and longevity in its respective use case.</p></p>
