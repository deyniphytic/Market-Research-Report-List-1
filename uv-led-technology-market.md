<p><strong>The UV LED Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The UV LED technology market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA LEDs, emitting wavelengths between 320-400 nm, are commonly used in curing applications and tanning. UVB LEDs, with wavelengths from 280-320 nm, are primarily utilized in phototherapy and industrial curing. UVC LEDs, emitting wavelengths below 280 nm, are essential for disinfection purposes, targeting bacteria and viruses. Each segment caters to distinct applications across healthcare, industrial, and consumer markets.</p></p>
