<p><p>The dental X-ray film market primarily features two main types: D-Speed X-ray film and Ultra-speed X-ray film. D-Speed X-ray film, known for its slower exposure time, requires higher radiation doses to produce images but offers decent detail. In contrast, Ultra-speed X-ray film significantly reduces exposure time, allowing for lower radiation doses while maintaining image quality. Both film types serve essential roles in dental diagnostics, catering to various clinical needs and ensuring effective patient care while balancing safety.</p></p>
